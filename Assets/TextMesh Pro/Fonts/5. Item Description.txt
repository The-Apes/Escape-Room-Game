Introducing **Molaser Brush - Ink Brush Font**

Molaser Brush - Ink Brush Font which has a hand lettering brush style. So it looks natural like a handmade. This texture is very detailed. This font is suitable for logos, product branding, printable templates or for text overlay to any background image, also suitable for use in title design. Such as apparel, invitations, books tittle, stationery design, quotes, branding, greeting card, t-shirt, packaging design, poster and more!

**FEATURES**
- TTF/OTF/WOFF
- Uppercase and Lowercase letters
- ligature
- Numbering and Punctuations
- Multilingual Supports
- Works on PC or Mac
- Simple Installation
- Support Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, also works on Microsoft Word
